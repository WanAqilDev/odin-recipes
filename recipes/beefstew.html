Slow Cooker Beef Tips and Gravy

Description

This slow cooker beef tips and gravy is perfect to serve over mashed potatoes, noodles, or rice. There's nothing more comforting for Sunday dinner than this hearty, yet tender beef dish.

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 8 servings
  • 2 teaspoons oil
  • 2 pounds beef steak tips
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cantanzaro herbs or Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup beef bone broth
  • 1(10.5 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup(98% fat-free 50% lower sodium)
  • 1(0.87 ounce) packet brown gravy mix
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms, cleaned and quartered

Directions

  1. Step 1


    Gather all ingredients

  2. Step 2


    Set a heavy cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat; add oil. Toss beef steak tips with salt, granulated garlic, Cantanzaro herbs, and pepper.

  3. Step 3


    Sear beef in batches until browned on all sides. Transfer steak tips to a slow cooker.

  4. Step 4


    Combine beef bone broth, soup, and dry gravy mix; pour over steak tips. Add mushrooms to the slow cooker.

  5. Step 5


    Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours or on High for 5 hours.
